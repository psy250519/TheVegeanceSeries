Old Boy

The second movie of vengeance series directed by Chan-wook Park who is one of the most popular directors.

Story: Dae-Su is an obnoxious drunk bailed from the police station yet again by a friend. However, he's abducted from the street and wakes up in a cell, where he remains for the next 15 years, drugged unconscious when human contact is unavoidable, otherwise with only the television as company. And then, suddenly released, he is invited to track down his jailor with a denouement that is simply stunning.

Initial release: November 21, 2003 (South Korea)
Director: Park Chan-wook
Language: Korean
Cinematography: Chung-hoon Chung
Awards: Hong Kong Film Award for Best Asian Film
