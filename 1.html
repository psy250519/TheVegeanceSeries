Sympathy for Mr.Vengeance

The first movie of vengeance series directed by Chan-wook Park who is one of the most popular directors.

Story: Deaf-mute Ryu d(Shin Ha-kyun) has just been fired from his job in a Seoul, South Korea, factory, and the timing couldn't be worse.His sister (Lim Ji-Eun) is suffering from kidney trouble, and she needs a transplant that Ryu himself can't provide.Ryu and his girlfriend (Bae Doo-na) then kidnap the daughter of wealthy tycoon Park (Song Kang-ho) to raise the money, but an unexpected disaster strikes, and Park launches a bloody quest for revenge that leaves many dead and puts Ryu's life in danger.

Initial release: March 29, 2002 (South Korea)
Director: Park Chan-wook
Cinematography: Kim Byeong-Il
Production company: CJ E&M Film Division
Screenplay: Park Chan-wook, Mu-yeong Lee, Jong-yong Lee, Lee Jae-soon
